Speakers & Worship

Voices for the weekend

Weekend One

Travis Hearn

Pastor · Author · NBA Team Chaplain

Senior Pastor of Impact Church in Scottsdale and chaplain for the Phoenix Suns. After surviving a life-threatening stroke, he carries a powerful testimony of perseverance — lived, not theorized.

Weekend One

Jason Patterson

Pastor · Author · Leadership Coach

Helped launch Parkside Church in Fishers, Indiana, and authored From Your Biggest Fan. Through storytelling, humor, and vulnerability, he helps men see leadership as a chance to strengthen and champion others.

Weekend Two

Travis Jones

Lead Pastor · Motivation Church

Church planter serving the Richmond, Virginia area for 13 years, built on a simple conviction: God can take broken people and build them back together. Over 20 years of ministry helping men rise with courage and live with purpose.

Weekend Two

Dr. Yisrael Vincent

AZAG Men's Director

Arizona Men's Director, theologian, and educator with a PhD in Biblical Interpretation. Direct, encouraging, and convinced God forms His strongest leaders through pressure.

Weekend One

John Petri

Lead Pastor · Reclaim Church

Leads Reclaim Church in Peoria with clarity, humility, and conviction — a steady pastoral voice calling men to take ownership of their faith and lead faithfully.

Weekend Two

Wendell McKinney

Founder · Divine Persuasion Studio

Entrepreneur and pastor bridging the Church and the marketplace — modeling what it looks like for men to live missionally as entrepreneurs, husbands, and fathers.
